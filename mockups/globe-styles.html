Globe Land/Water Differentiation Options

Choose a style for distinguishing land from water on the globe

Recommended

Option 1: Subtle Land Fill

Land masses have a subtle dark fill, water remains black. Clean and minimal.

Pros

  • Maintains dark aesthetic
  • Clear land/water distinction
  • Markers still pop

Cons

  • Subtle - may need adjustment
Atmospheric

Option 2: Ocean Depth Glow

Water has a deep blue tint, land stays dark. Creates depth effect.

Pros

  • Beautiful ocean depth effect
  • More atmospheric
  • Natural looking

Cons

  • May compete with cyan glow
  • Slightly busier
Current + Fill

Option 3: Filled Continents

Keep cyan borders, add semi-transparent fill to land masses.

Pros

  • Keeps existing border style
  • Very clear continents
  • Rich appearance

Cons

  • More visual complexity
  • May need border adjustment
